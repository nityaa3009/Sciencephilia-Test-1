Hello, I'm Dr. Bhavna.

Chemistry educator,
researcher, and learning partner.

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Chemical Sciences with a strong background in developing sustainable and innovative methods for industrial processes and the upscaling of biomass valorization. My research work includes published papers in catalysis, along with extensive experience in experimental techniques and data interpretation.

Over the past seven years, I have taught international school and university students across AP Chemistry, Chemistry Honors, General Chemistry, and college-level Organic Chemistry. I hold dual degrees in Science and Education, which helps me combine deep subject knowledge with teaching methods that are clear, structured, and effective.

I believe chemistry should feel simple, engaging, and accessible. Every learner brings unique strengths and learning styles, so I design individualized plans that help students move toward their academic goals with confidence and clarity.
