Hello, I'm Bhavna.

Chemistry educator,
mentor, and science enthusiast.

I've spent over eight years doing one thing — helping students build a genuine, lasting relationship with Chemistry. Not just memorizing formulas for an exam, but actually understanding the science behind the world around them.

My students come from the US, UK, Canada, UAE, Japan, Ghana, and over 20 other countries. Some are prepping for the SAT Subject Test. Some are struggling through a tough chapter. Some are just starting out in Grade 8. Every single session I teach is shaped entirely around that one student's goals, not a one-size-fits-all curriculum.

I hold an MSc in Chemistry and have been recognized as an award-winning educator in my field. But what I'm most proud of? A student who sends me a message saying they actually enjoy Chemistry now.
