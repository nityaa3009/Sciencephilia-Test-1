FAQ

Common Questions

What platform are sessions held on?
All sessions are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet. A stable internet connection and a device with a screen large enough to share documents comfortably is all you need.
Do you accommodate different time zones?
Yes. I work with students from the US, UK, Canada, UAE, Japan, and many more countries. Session times are agreed upon during our introductory call and can flex around your schedule.
Is there a trial or introductory session?
Yes — I offer a free 20-minute introductory call to understand your goals, level, and which course is the right fit. No commitment required. Just reach out via the form above.
What syllabi do you cover?
SAT Subject Test, IB Chemistry (SL and HL), IGCSE, A-Levels, AP Chemistry, and most national board syllabi. If you're unsure, mention it in your message and I'll confirm.
Can I switch courses mid-way?
Absolutely. If your needs change — say you start with doubt-clearing and want to move to a full one-on-one plan — that transition is easy and can be arranged with a week's notice.
How are payments handled?
Payment details are shared during onboarding. I accept international transfers, PayPal, and UPI. Monthly billing is standard; per-session billing is available for doubt-clearing.
